Les Pieds dans le Plat was created by a group of chefs and cooks in response to the Grenelle Environment Forum. The collective aims to transform food practices within collective catering by promoting meals that are 100% organic, homemade, and prepared with local and seasonal ingredients.

A central part of the initiative involves training and supporting kitchen teams working in institutions such as schools and retirement homes. Through this support, the collective helps these institutions adopt practices that prioritize organic and locally sourced food while maintaining practical and effective kitchen operations.

Les Pieds dans le Plat also works closely with local organic farming groups, public authorities, and other food-related organizations. By building these partnerships, the initiative seeks to strengthen networks that support food self-sufficiency and address issues such as malnutrition, particularly among vulnerable populations such as the elderly.
