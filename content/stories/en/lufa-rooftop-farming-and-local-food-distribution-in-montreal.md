Lufa is an urban farming initiative based in Montreal, founded in 2009 by four co-founders. The project was created to grow food closer to where people live, with the goal of increasing transparency in food production and promoting more sustainable food systems.

The initiative operates five rooftop greenhouses where food is produced in an urban environment. In addition to its own production, Lufa collaborates with local producers to offer a broader range of products.

Through an online marketplace, Lufa distributes these products directly to consumers. This model aims to reduce food waste, strengthen connections between producers and consumers, and support community engagement while prioritizing ethical and environmentally conscious practices.
