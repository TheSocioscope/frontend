# Urban Agriculture in Tokyo

In one of the world's most densely populated cities, a growing movement is transforming rooftops into productive gardens, reconnecting urbanites with their food sources.

## The Challenge

Tokyo faces unique challenges for sustainable food systems:
- Limited space for traditional agriculture
- Dependence on distant food supplies
- Disconnection between consumers and food production
- Urban heat island effects

## The Innovation

Community groups and businesses are converting unused rooftop space into productive gardens that provide fresh vegetables, create green spaces, and build community connections.
