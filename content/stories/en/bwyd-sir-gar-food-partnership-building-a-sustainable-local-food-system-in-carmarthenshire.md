The Bwyd Sir Gar Food Partnership was created during the COVID-19 pandemic in Carmarthenshire, West Wales. The initiative emerged to strengthen local collaboration around food and to address challenges related to food access and resilience.

As a member of the Sustainable Food Places network, the partnership works with a range of communities and stakeholders to develop a more diverse, inclusive, and sustainable food system. Its activities are built on cooperation and community engagement.

The partnership focuses on projects that improve access to food, encourage local sourcing, and empower disadvantaged communities. Through these efforts, it aims to ensure that everyone has access to healthy and sustainable food while strengthening community participation and resilience.
