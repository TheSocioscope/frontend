question: Who leads The Socioscope project?
order: 5
---

The Socioscope is led by two world-renowned research institutions:

**Complexity Science Hub Vienna (CSH)**
- Project Lead: Helga Nowotny
- Bringing expertise in complexity science and social systems

**Paris Institute for Advanced Study (IAS)**
- Project Lead: Saadi Lahlou
- Contributing insights from social psychology and behavioral science

The project is supported by the NOMIS Foundation and involves a global network of researchers, practitioners, and community partners across 29 countries.
