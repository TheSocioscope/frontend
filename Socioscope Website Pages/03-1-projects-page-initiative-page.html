About Dudu Masters

Dudu Masters is an innovative initiative focused on sustainable farming through insect farming, particularly black soldier flies and red earthworms. Founded by Jennifer, a trained entomologist, the project transforms organic waste into valuable resources like organic fertilizer and animal feed.

Dudu Masters also offers training and starter kits to empower farmers and households, promoting a circular economy that enhances soil health and food security while reducing reliance on synthetic fertilizers.
