Quick Facts

Essential information about The Socioscope at a glance

What We Do

The Socioscope explores how sustainable change happens on the ground, with a focus on food systems. We document local solutions, speak with practitioners, and analyze insights using AI to reveal the patterns behind impactful innovation.

Our Mission

A transformative qualitative research project that maps how behaviours, norms, and communities influence the transition toward sustainable food systems globally.

Key Information

  • Founded: 2022
  • Headquarters: Paris, France
  • Research Reach: 36+ countries
  • Website: thesocioscope.org

Our Audience

  • Environmentally engaged professionals
  • Researchers and academics
  • Practitioners in sustainable food systems
  • Policymakers and decision-makers
